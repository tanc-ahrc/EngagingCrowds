Information on reproduction

The charts were generated by scripts at https://github.com/nationalarchives/engagingcrowds_engagement.git.

A reproduction recipe is included in the README.txt in the downloads, above.

Modifying that recipe to use commit 97cedb41511b9494b750152b26d91cfe24bd46d1 will produce the charts shown in the report.

The charts should not change significantly with later commits, but new charts may be added or bugs may be fixed.
